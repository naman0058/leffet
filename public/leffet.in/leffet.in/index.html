About Us

L’effet personifies class, tradition, and grandeur of the art we call fashion.
Indian weaves, created in diverse fabrics with detailed embroideries and traditional block prints have defined L'effets’ journey over the past 26 Years. Sanjev Marwaaha’s passion and admiration towards these weaves has been one of the main reasons behind L'effets’ fine masterpieces.

Leffet's Art Elements

Embroidery isn’t just about a needle and thread, it's about the colours, shades & tones used with multiple techniques that are designed to adorn the garment like a fine piece of artwork.

The artworks we use for embroideries are original, designed and developed by our in-house team of artists who are inspired by crafts and arts from all across our country. And we generally use multiple materials to give the embroideries a 3-dimensional look.
